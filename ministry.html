Our Ministries

At Encounter Victory Church, we are committed to fostering a strong sense of community and spiritual growth through our dedicated ministries. Each ministry is designed to support and enrich the lives of our congregation members. Here is an overview of the vibrant ministries we offer:

Youth Ministry

Our Youth Ministry is dedicated to nurturing the spiritual growth of our young members. We offer a vibrant and engaging environment where teenagers can connect, learn, and grow in their faith. Through dynamic worship, Bible studies, and community service projects, we aim to inspire and empower the next generation to live out their faith boldly.

Men Ministry

The Men’s Ministry at Encounter Victory Church is a place for men of all ages to come together, support one another, and grow spiritually. We provide opportunities for fellowship, discipleship, and service through various events, Bible studies, and outreach programs. Our goal is to encourage men to lead lives of integrity, strength, and purpose.

Ladies’ Ministry

Our Ladies’ Ministry is designed to support and uplift the women in our church community. Through Bible studies, prayer groups, social events, and service opportunities, we seek to create a nurturing environment where women can deepen their faith and build lasting friendships.
