About Church

The Encounter Victory Church(EVC) is a family Church founded in 2014 by Apostle Samuel Mburu Kamau who serves as chairman and lead pastor in the EVC churches. The church is domiciled in the Mihango area of Utawala Estate in Nairobi County.

EVC is registered as a church by the Registrar of societies. Utawala estate is a relatively young residential Estate having emerged as a settlement of choice after the construction of the Eastern by-pass. It is a fast growing neighborhood attracting large numbers of young working populations and families.

It was in this backdrop that an idea of a family church was conceived to serve the growing population. In addition the Estate has a big population of adult youths who are in need of spiritual help for direction.
